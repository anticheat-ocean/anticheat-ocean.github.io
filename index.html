Capabilities

Built for decisions, not dashboards.

Every part of Ocean is designed to reduce interpretation time: fewer vanity metrics, clearer evidence, and enough context to make a defensible call.

01

Evidence, ordered by relevance

Suspicious artifacts are grouped and prioritized so reviewers start with what matters instead of digging through raw system output.

02

A readable forensic trail

Execution history, memory signals, registry entries, and event traces stay connected to their source and timestamp.

03

Reports that stay controlled

Case data is presented as a structured report, keeping operational noise away from the people making enforcement decisions.

04
Ready for review

Context before enforcement

Ocean labels risk and explains why a finding matters. The tool supports the reviewer; it does not replace staff judgment.

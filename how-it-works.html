The Ocean workflow

From machine noise
to a defensible verdict.

Ocean keeps collection, interpretation, and enforcement separate—so a strong signal gets attention without turning an automated score into an automatic decision.

01

Acquire

Start with a clean collection.

Download the current Windows build and run the checks defined by your staff workflow. Ocean gathers relevant system evidence without making the verdict for you.

Direct release downloadConsistent collection pathNo account interstitial
02

Prioritize

Separate signal from background noise.

Memory indicators, execution artifacts, registry traces, startup entries, and events are grouped by source and surfaced with practical severity labels.

Source-aware findingsTimestamped evidenceCritical / review / clean
03

Review

Read the context before the score.

A confidence score can point staff in the right direction, but the finding details explain what happened and why it deserves attention.

Human-readable contextEvidence-first layoutStaff-led interpretation
04

Decide

Apply your own enforcement policy.

Ocean supports a consistent review. Your staff remains responsible for the final decision, case notes, and any action taken within the community.

Policy stays with staffExplainable decisionsConsistent case handling

Design principle

Automation finds. People decide.

Anti-cheat evidence is rarely useful without context. Ocean's job is to make the review smaller, faster, and more legible—not to hide uncertainty behind a dramatic number.

You've got answers

Answer your questions

Ocean helps staff teams detect cheating users through fast forensic scans, maintained detections, and case reports that are easy to review during a screenshare.

The scanner supports Windows operating systems, including Windows 7, 8, 10, and 11, plus Linux distributions for supported workflows.

Ocean securely stores scan activity, timestamps, program usage signals, system activity, and forensic indicators needed to review suspicious behavior.

The static demo does not process payments, but the product presentation supports credit cards, PayPal, bank transfer, crypto, and regional payment methods.

A standard scan is designed to complete in about 60 seconds, depending on the suspect machine and enabled custom rules.
